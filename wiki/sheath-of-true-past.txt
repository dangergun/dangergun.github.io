Sheath of True Past [0x61d3]
"Made from regrets of the past as to not lose once more what has already been lost."
Legendary tier sheath.

MP Cost: 120.
Cooldown: 0.5 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
On self: Armored for 5 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Berserk for 5 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
While ability key is held, Berserk on self and drains 10 mp/second.
When ability key is released, 1450 damage within 5 tiles. Uses atk mod.

While equipped:
+15 Defense.
+15 Restoration.
+20% Dexterity.

Does not drop from enemies.
