Blades of Majesty [0x6706]
"Forged from the purest platinum for the mightiest of kings."
Legendary tier blades.

Does 140-155 damage.
105% rate of fire.
Shoots 3 projectiles at a 8.0 degree angle.
Range: 3.9 tiles.
Shots have an amplitude of 0.25 and a frequency of 1.
Shots ignore defense.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

While equipped:
+110 Maximum HP.
+5 Attack.
+5 Dexterity.
-10 Defense.
-70 Maximum MP.

Slayer Recovery Technique: If your surge is at least 10 when using your ability you gain health based on how much surge you have.

Does not drop from enemies.
