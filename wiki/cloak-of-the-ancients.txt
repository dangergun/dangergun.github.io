Cloak of the Ancients [0x49b2]
"A drape worn by the greatest thief throughout all ages, it's tailoring is truly exquisite."
Legendary tier cloak.

MP Cost: 120.
Cooldown: 9.0 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
Effect on self: +50 Vitality for 6 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Damaging for 5.5 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Invisible for 8 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
Teleports the player to the cursor, with a maximum distance of 20 tiles.

While equipped:
+20 Might.
+5 Speed.
+8 Defense.

Does not drop from enemies.
