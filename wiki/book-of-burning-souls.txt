Book of Burning Souls [0x61d2]
"This tome is forged from the souls of hell's rejects in order to bring revenge upon the underworld."
Legendary tier tome.

MP Cost: 50.
Cooldown: 0.5 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
On self: Sick for 3 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
Party effect: Armored for 3 seconds within 6 tiles. Uses wis mod.

While equipped:
+25% Attack.

True Inferno: Using this ability explodes and burns enemies in a radius of 4 squares around you. This explosion does 8000 damage over 8 seconds.

Does not drop from enemies.
