Shooting Star [0x7174]
"A brilliant star forged from a solar flare, it's light showing no signs of diminishing soon."
Sacred tier shuriken.

Does 950-1050 damage.
Range: 10.0 tiles.
Shots hit multiple targets.
Shots pass through obstacles.
MP Cost: 60.
Cooldown: 2.0 seconds.

When used:
On self: Bravery for 2 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
While ability key is held, Speedy on self and drains 10 mp/second.
When ability key is released, shoots a shuriken. Does not use atk mod.

While equipped:
+10 Might.
+30 Luck.
+5 Speed.
-10 Attack.

Critical Focus: On enemy hit, 7% chance to increase might by 10% of total luck. Stackable.

Does not drop from enemies.
