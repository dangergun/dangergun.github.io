Dark Conflagration Spell [0x1643]
"A spell created to hold a calamitous inferno within time, unbinding the temporal locks unleashes a fraction of its destructive power. WARNING: Fatigue may be caused after use of this spell."
Legendary tier spell.

Does 410-570 damage.
Range: 3.0 tiles.
Shots have an amplitude of 2.0 and a frequency of 6.
MP Cost: 160.
Cooldown: 0.5 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 6%.

When used:
On self: Unstable for 2 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
Shoots 12 shots in a circle around the cursor.

While equipped:
-80 Maximum HP.

Does not drop from enemies.
