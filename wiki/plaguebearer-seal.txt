Plaguebearer Seal [0x7087]
"A seal held by the cosmic beings above the realm, how did it get here?"
Sacred tier seal.

MP Cost: 90.
Cooldown: 4.0 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
Enemy Effect: Armor Broken for 4 seconds within 4 tiles. Centered around the player's cursor.
On self: Berserk for 4 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Sick for 3 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
Party effect: Damaging for 6 seconds within 6 tiles. Does not use wis mod.
Party effect: Within 6 tiles, +165 Maximum HP for 4 seconds. Does not use wis mod.

While equipped:
+2 Attack.
+2 Dexterity.
-10 Restoration.
-5 Defense.
-5 Speed.

Collector's Edition: All stats are increased by 0.65% per sacred item in inventory. Stackable.

Does not drop from enemies.
