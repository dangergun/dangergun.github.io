Twilight Star [0x7250]
"Crafted from the remains of a fallen star, this weapon proves to be deadly, while shining beautifully."
Sacred tier shuriken.

Does 500-1000 damage.
Range: 15.0 tiles.
Shots pass through obstacles.
MP Cost: 50.
Cooldown: 2.0 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
On self: Berserk for 2 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Damaging for 2 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Speedy for 2 seconds. Does not use wis mod.

Critical Focus: On enemy hit, 7% chance to increase might by 10% of total luck. Stackable.

Drops from:
lod Ivory Wyvern: 0.2% (1/500) chance.
