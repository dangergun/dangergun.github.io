Bottled Mercury [0x7874]
"A sturdy glass bottle containing a potent poison, if anyone were to get a hold of this mercury many lives would be lost..."
Sacred tier poison.

MP Cost: 300.
Cooldown: 0.5 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
Enemy Effect: Slowed for 6 seconds within 2 tiles. Centered around the player's cursor.
Enemy Effect: Weak for 3 seconds within 2.5 tiles. Centered around the player's cursor.
On self: Berserk for 2.5 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Bleeding for 2.5 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
Poison Grenade:
After 2.5 seconds, 2200 impact damage + 7000 damage over 8 seconds. Uses wis mod.

While equipped:
+15 Wisdom.
+8 Speed.
-10 Vitality.
-70 Maximum HP.

Runic Defense: On hit, 8% chance to trigger an AOE heal for the taken damage with a 4 tile radius. Stackable.

Drops from:
True Somnus, the Dream Entity: 0.2% (1/500) chance.
