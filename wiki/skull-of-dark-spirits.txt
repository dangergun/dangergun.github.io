Skull of Dark Spirits [0x585b]
"Said to suck the life out of foes in order to provide the user with everlasting life."
Legendary tier skull.

MP Cost: 125.
Cooldown: 0.5 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
Enemy Effect: Curse for 2 seconds within 3 tiles. Centered around the player's cursor.
Party effect: Healing for 5 seconds within 4 tiles. Does not use wis mod.
Steals 500 HP within 6.5 tiles. Centered around the player's cursor.

While equipped:
+40 Luck.
+40 Might.
+8 Attack.
+8 Dexterity.

Does not drop from enemies.
