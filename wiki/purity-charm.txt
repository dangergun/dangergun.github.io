Purity Charm [0x7194]
"A charm with the ability to summon a blood alter at will, a blood jewel protecting it."
Sacred tier charm.

MP Cost: 110.
Cooldown: 6.0 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
Spawns a defensive Torii.
Within 5 tiles: Damaging for 3 seconds.
Disappears after 5 seconds.
Spawns an offensive Torii.
Within 4 tiles: ArmorBroken for 4 seconds.
Disappears after 4 seconds.

While equipped:
+5 Attack.
+5% Defense.
-5 Speed.
-60 Maximum MP.

Runic Offense: On hit, 8% chance to trigger an AOE damage for 1.5x of taken damage with a 4 tile radius. Stackable.

Does not drop from enemies.
