Haunted Talisman [0x7189]
"This Talisman holds the ghost of its former self, forever trapped inside."
Sacred tier talisman.

MP Cost: 100.
Cooldown: 3.0 seconds.

When used:
On self: Berserk for 2 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Sick for 1.5 seconds. Does not use wis mod.

While equipped:
+10 Attack.
+20 Restoration.
-10 Vitality.
-20 Protection.
-50 Maximum HP.

Spawns a ghost to aid you in combat.
Damage: 1300
Lifetime: 4.2
Effect: Cursed for 2 secs

Runic Offense: On hit, 8% chance to trigger an AOE damage for 1.5x of taken damage with a 4 tile radius. Stackable.

Does not drop from enemies.
