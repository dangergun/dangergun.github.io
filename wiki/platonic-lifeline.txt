Platonic Lifeline [0x7050]
"Once a tablet of medicinal power but now is overrun by a unwelcome force creating nothing but fear."
Sacred tier spell.

Does 120-240 damage.
Range: 6.0 tiles.
Shots have an amplitude of 1.25 and a frequency of 1.
MP Cost: 110.
Cooldown: 0.5 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
On self: Berserk for 2 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Healing for 2 seconds. Uses wis mod.
Shoots 12 shots in a circle around the cursor.

While equipped:
+20 Vitality.
+300 Maximum HP.
-20 Wisdom.
-6 Dexterity.
-6 Speed.

Galactic Valor: All status effect durations are increased by 5%. Stackable.

Does not drop from enemies.
