Purity's Call [0x47be]
"A seal made from heavenly essence in order to protect and purify the masses."
Legendary tier seal.

MP Cost: 120.
Cooldown: 3.0 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
On self: Invulnerable for 1.5 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
Party effect: Damaging for 8 seconds within 7.5 tiles. Does not use wis mod.
Party effect: Within 4.5 tiles, +100 Maximum HP for 4 seconds. Uses wis mod.

While equipped:
+100 Maximum HP.
+25 Protection.
+6 Defense.
-10 Restoration.

Does not drop from enemies.
