Bloodstone of Enmity [0x6814]
"A rock found near the grave of Drannol. It seems to hold a great amount of power."
Legendary tier consumable.

When used:
On self: Berserk for 7 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Bravery for 7 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Slowed for 7 seconds. Does not use wis mod.

Drops from:
Drannol, the Eternal Beast: 0.1% (1/1000) chance.
Ultra Drannol, the Eternal Beast: 0.2% (1/500) chance.
