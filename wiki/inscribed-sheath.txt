Inscribed Sheath [0x7238]
"A scribes blade hidden behind a sealed sheath to protect the user from its runes calling for vengeance against its creator."
Sacred tier sheath.

MP Cost: 90.
Cooldown: 0.5 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
On self: Damaging for 4 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Surged for 4 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
While ability key is held, Berserk on self and drains 10 mp/second.
When ability key is released, 4000 damage within 4 tiles. Uses atk mod.

While equipped:
+10 Vitality.
+100 Maximum HP.
-50 Restoration.

+2 Life Steal.

Helping Hand: On ability use, 7% chance to give 10 attack and 10 defense to each player in a 3 tile radius for 5 seconds. Stackable.

Does not drop from enemies.
