Skull of the Titan [0x47d8]
"A helmet made from the cranium of the titan, which grants the wearer a powerful defence."
Legendary tier helm.

MP Cost: 100.
Cooldown: 10.0 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
On self: Armored for 4.5 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Speedy for 6 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
Party effect: Berserk for 9 seconds within 7 tiles. Uses wis mod.

While equipped:
+150 Maximum HP.
+35 Vitality.

+1 Life Steal.

Affliction: If you are attacked by an enemy you deal half the damage back to them.

Does not drop from enemies.
