Dice of Resistance [0x7184]
"A gold forged dice used to gamble away morale during the dark ages for Aldridge, a memorabilia not to be lost."
Sacred tier dice.

MP Cost: 125.
Cooldown: 4.5 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
Grants Berserk, Armored, or Stunned for 7 seconds.
On self: Invulnerable for 1.5 seconds. Does not use wis mod.

While equipped:
+20 Might.
+50 Luck.
+80 Maximum HP.

Critical Focus: On enemy hit, 7% chance to increase might by 10% of total luck. Stackable.

Does not drop from enemies.
