Spectres Shroud [0x7095]
"Clouded by a dark mist, this cloak is said to be used by the Grim Reaper when collecting souls"
Sacred tier cloak.

MP Cost: 140.
Cooldown: 5.0 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:

On self: Damaging for 3 seconds. Does not use wis mod.

While equipped:
+10 Vitality.
+15 Wisdom.
+5 Attack.
-5 Speed.

Adrenaline Rush: On hit, 8% chance to ignore incoming damage. Stackable.

Does not drop from enemies.
