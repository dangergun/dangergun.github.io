Marble Seal [0x5477]
"A seal made from the Marble Colossus' head. The power of a pure and mighty paladin still remains within."
Legendary tier seal.

MP Cost: 95.
Cooldown: 4.5 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
Party effect: Armored for 4 seconds within 3 tiles. Does not use wis mod.
Party effect: Bravery for 3 seconds within 3 tiles. Does not use wis mod.
Party effect: Healing for 3 seconds within 3 tiles. Does not use wis mod.

While equipped:
+10 Defense.

Marbelize: You have a chance to activate Marbelize every few seconds, which increases HP by 150, ATK and DEF by 25 (Stackable). 

Drops from:
LH Marble Colossus: 0.2% (1/500) chance.
