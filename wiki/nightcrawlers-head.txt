Nightcrawler's Head [0x7234]
"The realm used to be peaceful and caring until the shade fell, this is all that's left of their attempt at domination."
Sacred tier skull.

MP Cost: 100.
Cooldown: 0.5 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
Enemy Effect: Armor Broken for 4 seconds within 3 tiles. Centered around the player's cursor.
Enemy Effect: Slowed for 2.5 seconds within 4 tiles. Centered around the player's cursor.
Enemy Effect: Stunned for 1.7 seconds within 2.5 tiles. Centered around the player's cursor.
On self: Berserk for 2 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
Steals 250 HP within 6 tiles. Centered around the player's cursor.

While equipped:
+5 Dexterity.

+1 Mana Leech.

Collector's Edition: All stats are increased by 0.65% per sacred item in inventory. Stackable.

Does not drop from enemies.
