Malgoric Charm [0x673b]
"A charm used to summon a Torii charged with the malicious power of Malgor."
Untiered charm.

MP Cost: 120.
Cooldown: 3.0 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 6%.

On self: Berserk for 3 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
Spawns an offensive Torii.
Within 6 tiles: Bleeding for 6 seconds.
Disappears after 6 seconds.

While equipped:
+100 Maximum HP.

Drops from:
Malgor, the Eternal Fiend: 5.0% (1/20) chance.
