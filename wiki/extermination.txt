Extermination [0x47ca]
"Used to destroy everything in sight, this abilities power is unparalleled."
Legendary tier spell.

Does 300-360 damage.
Range: 6.0 tiles.
Shots hit multiple targets.
Shots ignore defense.
MP Cost: 125.
Cooldown: 2.0 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
On self: Berserk for 2.5 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Speedy for 3 seconds. Uses wis mod.
Shoots 12 shots in a circle around the cursor.

While equipped:
+15 Speed.
+8 Attack.

Does not drop from enemies.
