Gem of True Eminence [0x511a]
"Shrouded with many mysteries, this prism is said to be the essence of everything that is imperceptible. "
Legendary tier prism.

MP Cost: 140.
Cooldown: 6.5 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
Creates a decoy that lasts for 6.25 seconds.
On self: Invisible for 6 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Invulnerable for 2.4 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
Teleports the player to the cursor, with a maximum distance of 20 tiles.

While equipped:
+100 Maximum HP.
+4 Speed.
+8 Defense.

Does not drop from enemies.
