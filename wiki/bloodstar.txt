Bloodstar [0x47f5]
"Made from the blood of innocent lives, cries can be heard from this dreadful star."
Legendary tier shuriken.

Does 1150-1200 damage.
Range: 16.5 tiles.
Shots hit multiple targets.
Shots ignore defense.
MP Cost: 105.
Cooldown: 0.5 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
On self: Berserk for 4 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Surged for 4 seconds. Does not use wis mod.

While equipped:
+5 Speed.
+6 Attack.
+7 Vitality.

Does not drop from enemies.
