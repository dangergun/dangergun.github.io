Lance of Immense Protection [0x69e5]
"Wielding this enchants the user with a safeguard."
Legendary tier lance.

Does 50-100 damage.
80% rate of fire.
Shoots 4 projectiles at a 4.0 degree angle.
Range: 5.22 tiles.
Shots have an amplitude of 2.0 and a frequency of 1.
Shots ignore defense.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

While equipped:
+10 Restoration.
+120 Maximum HP.
+30 Protection.
+6 Speed.

Blessing of the Skies: If your protection is depleted your surge increases by 15 everytime you use an ability.

Does not drop from enemies.
