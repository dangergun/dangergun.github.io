Sheath Of Dark Deeds [0x7239]
"Most sheaths simply hide a blade within, this one hides its dark intentions..."
Sacred tier sheath.

MP Cost: 80.
Cooldown: 0.5 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
On self: Armored for 5 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Sick for 3.5 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Speedy for 5 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
Party effect: Empowered for 3 seconds within 3 tiles. Does not use wis mod.
While ability key is held, Berserk on self and drains 10 mp/second.
When ability key is released, 1500 damage within 7 tiles. Uses atk mod.

While equipped:
+10 Wisdom.
+100 Maximum MP.
-15 Vitality.
-5 Attack.
-5 Dexterity.

Galactic Valor: All status effect durations are increased by 5%. Stackable.

Does not drop from enemies.
