Prism of Mirrors [0x6120]
"A prism constructed in ancient times, its endless reflections predict the user’s demise."
Legendary tier prism.

MP Cost: 100.
Cooldown: 0.5 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
Creates a decoy that lasts for 3 seconds.
On self: Damaging for 1 second. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Speedy for 2 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
Teleports the player to the cursor, with a maximum distance of 20 tiles.

While equipped:
+10 Protection.
+20 Restoration.
+8 Dexterity.

Dimensional Assault: If you have over 10 surge when you use this ability you do not lose any mana.

Does not drop from enemies.
