Death's Vow [0x6202]
"A cursed charm that drains the energy from your soul but in return will send opposers to oblivion."
Legendary tier charm.

MP Cost: 130.
Cooldown: 4.5 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
Spawns an offensive Torii.
Within 6 tiles: Bleeding for 5 seconds.
Disappears after 3 seconds.
Trap: 400 damage within 3 tiles.
On enemies: Curse for 3 seconds.
Throw time: 1 second.

Does not drop from enemies.
