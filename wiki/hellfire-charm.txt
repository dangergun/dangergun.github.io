Hellfire Charm [0x7193]
"A charm said to have the ability to open the gateway to hell… why would you want this in your possession?"
Sacred tier charm.

MP Cost: 90.
Cooldown: 5.0 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
On self: Berserk for 3 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
Spawns an offensive Torii.
Within 3 tiles: Weak for 3 seconds.
Disappears after 3 seconds.

While equipped:
+10 Dexterity.

Collector's Edition: All stats are increased by 0.65% per sacred item in inventory. Stackable.

Does not drop from enemies.
