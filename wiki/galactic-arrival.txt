Galactic Arrival [0x7092]
"An orb given to the people of the realm upon their arrival, seemingly watching our every move."
Sacred tier orb.

MP Cost: 110.
Cooldown: 5.5 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
Enemy Effect: Dazed for 6 seconds within 6 tiles. Centered around the player's cursor.
Enemy Effect: Slowed for 6 seconds within 6 tiles. Centered around the player's cursor.
Enemy effect: Within 3 tiles of the player's cursor, Stasis for 3.5 seconds.
On self: Armored for 5 seconds. Uses wis mod.
On self: Speedy for 4 seconds. Uses wis mod.

While equipped:
+6 Defense.
+6 Dexterity.
+8 Speed.
-40 Maximum HP.
-5 Wisdom.

Galactic Valor: All status effect durations are increased by 5%. Stackable.

Does not drop from enemies.
