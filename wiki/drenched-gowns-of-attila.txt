Drenched Gowns of Attila [0x7094]
"A gown that once represented royalty, now stained with grief and shame."
Sacred tier cloak.

MP Cost: 110.
Cooldown: 5.5 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
On self: Berserk for 3 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Invisible for 5 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Speedy for 4 seconds. Does not use wis mod.

While equipped:
+10 Speed.
+50% Protection.
-6 Wisdom.

Collector's Edition: All stats are increased by 0.65% per sacred item in inventory. Stackable.

Does not drop from enemies.
