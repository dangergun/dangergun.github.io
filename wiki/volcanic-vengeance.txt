Volcanic Vengeance [0x61da]
"Built from the most scorching magma, it is said to deal massive amounts of damage upon the users enemies."
Legendary tier scepter.

MP Cost: 90.
Cooldown: 0.5 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
Enemy Effect: Armor Broken for 2 seconds within 3 tiles. Centered around the player's cursor.
Lightning: 1200 damage to 6 targets. Uses wis mod.
On self: Damaging for 2 seconds. Uses wis mod.

While equipped:
+2 Life Steal.

Scorch: The extreme heat of this scepter's energy causes damage melts away the defenses of enemies.

Does not drop from enemies.
