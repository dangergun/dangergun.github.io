Banner of the Ages [0x7197]
"A banner painted with the emblems of those that participated in the wars sworn to be forgotten"
Sacred tier banner.

MP Cost: 100.
Cooldown: 5.0 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
On self: Armored for 3 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Damaging for 3 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Sick for 4 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
Within 8 tiles, empowers allies for 7 seconds. Stays active for 5 seconds.

While equipped:
+10 Protection.
+5 Defense.
-5 Vitality.

Collector's Edition: All stats are increased by 0.65% per sacred item in inventory. Stackable.

Does not drop from enemies.
