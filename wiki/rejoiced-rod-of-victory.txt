Rejoiced Rod of Victory [0x7180]
"A lonesome sorcerer's scepter used to keep the evil forces at bay during the final stand."
Sacred tier scepter.

MP Cost: 130.
Cooldown: 2.5 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
Lightning: 800 damage to 8 targets. Uses wis mod.
Lightning effect: Weak for 2.5 seconds.
On self: Berserk for 1 second. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Slowed for 2 seconds. Does not use wis mod.

While equipped:
+20 Wisdom.
+5 Vitality.
+85 Maximum MP.
-5 Defense.

Open Mind: Mana regeneration is increased by 10% and maximum mana is increased by 5%. Stackable.

Does not drop from enemies.
