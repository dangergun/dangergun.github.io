Burden of the Warpawn [0x48fe]
"The end will be forever if we don't stop this war. ~Vardon, the Remembered"
Legendary tier helm.

MP Cost: 120.
Cooldown: 8.0 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
Enemy Effect: Stunned for 4 seconds within 5 tiles. Centered around the player's cursor.
Heals 200 HP within 4 tiles. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Berserk for 5 seconds. Does not use wis mod.

While equipped:
+10 Defense.
+10 Dexterity.
+10 Vitality.
+120 Maximum HP.

Does not drop from enemies.
