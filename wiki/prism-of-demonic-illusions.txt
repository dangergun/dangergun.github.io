Prism of Demonic Illusions [0x7341]
"A lustrous red prism luring those vulnerable enough into a future of sorrow and eternal regret."
Untiered prism.

Cooldown: 2.5 seconds.

+8 Dexterity.
-60 Maximum HP.

Drops from:
Oryx Defense System: 0.5% (1/200) chance.
