Ancient Aztec Skull [0x7235]
"The mayans believe the end of the world was to come very soon, their predictions are truly something to fear."
Sacred tier skull.

MP Cost: 110.
Cooldown: 0.5 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:

On self: Damaging for 3 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
Party effect: Surged for 2 seconds within 3.5 tiles. Does not use wis mod.
Steals 350 HP within 5.5 tiles. Centered around the player's cursor.

While equipped:
+3 Vitality.
+6 Attack.
+6 Dexterity.
-4 Wisdom.
-8 Restoration.

Runic Defense: On hit, 8% chance to trigger an AOE heal for the taken damage with a 4 tile radius. Stackable.

Does not drop from enemies.
