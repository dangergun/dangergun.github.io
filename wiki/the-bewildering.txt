The Bewildering [0x46d8]
"A sphere of energy formed from the depths of the earth, it grants vast strength in order to overcome the users foe."
Legendary tier orb.

MP Cost: 110.
Cooldown: 5.0 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
Enemy Effect: Curse for 2 seconds within 4 tiles. Centered around the player's cursor.
Enemy effect: Within 3 tiles of the player's cursor, Stasis for 5 seconds.
On self: Armored for 4 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Damaging for 3 seconds. Uses wis mod.

While equipped:
+4 Vitality.
+6 Defense.
+6 Speed.

Timeplosion: This orb has double the radius of a regular orb.

Does not drop from enemies.
