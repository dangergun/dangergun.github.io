Judgement's Call [0x1483]
"A prism made to decimate the users enemies, leaving no margin of error. "
Legendary tier prism.

MP Cost: 110.
Cooldown: 1.1 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
Creates a decoy that lasts for 7 seconds.
Enemy Effect: Curse for 7 seconds within 3.5 tiles. Centered around the player.
Enemy Effect: Slowed for 7 seconds within 2.5 tiles. Centered around the player.
On self: Berserk for 7 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
Teleports the player to the cursor, with a maximum distance of 20 tiles.

While equipped:
+11 Wisdom.
+7 Attack.
+7 Vitality.

Does not drop from enemies.
