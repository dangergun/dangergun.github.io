Cursed Anomaly Orb [0x7093]
"An amethyst orb set upon its base, the twinkling light appears to come from within."
Sacred tier orb.

MP Cost: 120.
Cooldown: 5.0 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
Enemy Effect: Curse for 8 seconds within 6 tiles. Centered around the player's cursor.
Enemy effect: Within 3 tiles of the player's cursor, Stasis for 5 seconds.
On self: Berserk for 2.5 seconds. Uses wis mod.
On self: Damaging for 2.5 seconds. Uses wis mod.
On self: Sick for 2 seconds. Uses wis mod.
On self: Slowed for 3 seconds. Uses wis mod.

While equipped:
-60 Maximum HP.
-60 Maximum MP.

+1 Mana Leech.

Collector's Edition: All stats are increased by 0.65% per sacred item in inventory. Stackable.

Does not drop from enemies.
