Dice of the Scriptures [0x7183]
"A dice imbued with the power written in the ancient Aldridge language leaving it's tale, a mystery left to unfold."
Sacred tier dice.

MP Cost: 90.
Cooldown: 3.0 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
Grants Dazed, Damaging, or Berserk for 6 seconds.
On self: Bravery for 3 seconds. Does not use wis mod.

While equipped:
+50 Luck.

Galactic Valor: All status effect durations are increased by 5%. Stackable.

Does not drop from enemies.
