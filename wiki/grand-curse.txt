Grand Curse [0x69e9]
"In order to protect the realm from certain doom, this spell was crafted to trap those who are tainted."
Legendary tier spell.

Does 290-340 damage.
Range: 6.0 tiles.
Shots hit multiple targets.
Shots pass through obstacles.
Shots ignore defense.
MP Cost: 100.
Cooldown: 0.5 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
On self: Armored for 1.4 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
Shoots 12 shots in a circle around the cursor.

While equipped:
+10 Luck.
+10 Restoration.
+200 Maximum MP.

Expansion: When your Surge is at least 50 you gain bonus mana.

Does not drop from enemies.
