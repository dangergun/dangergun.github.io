The Storm Blanket [0x179c]
"A cloak formed by thousands of lighting bolts that protect the user from danger."
Legendary tier cloak.

MP Cost: 115.
Cooldown: 4.5 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
On self: Invisible for 4.5 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Invulnerable for 1 second. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Slowed for 2.5 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
On self: Speedy for 4 seconds. Does not use wis mod.

While equipped:
+6 Defense.
-9 Speed.
Grants Armored after 75 seconds.

Does not drop from enemies.
