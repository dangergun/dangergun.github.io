Spell of 1000 Strikes [0x48fd]
"Formed by the very bolts in the sky in order to bestow its enemies with wraith and destruction."
Legendary tier spell.

Does 200-280 damage.
Range: 6.0 tiles.
Shots hit multiple targets.
Shots pass through obstacles.
Shots are parametric.
MP Cost: 140.
Cooldown: 2.0 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
Shoots 12 shots in a circle around the cursor.

While equipped:
+10 Attack.
+10 Dexterity.
+100 Maximum HP.
+100 Maximum MP.

Does not drop from enemies.
