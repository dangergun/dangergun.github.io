Helm of the Unholy Warrior [0x7091]
"A Helm hand sculpted from the carcusses of those that a ravageous warrior exiled."
Sacred tier helm.

MP Cost: 100.
Cooldown: 6.0 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
On self: Bravery for 5 seconds. Does not use wis mod.
Party effect: Berserk for 5 seconds within 5 tiles. Does not use wis mod.

While equipped:
+50 Luck.
-5 Speed.

+1 Mana Leech.

Helping Hand: On ability use, 7% chance to give 10 attack and 10 defense to each player in a 3 tile radius for 5 seconds. Stackable.

Does not drop from enemies.
