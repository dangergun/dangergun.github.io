Fortification Shield [0x63a7]
"An enormous tower-shield whose design is taken from the entry doors of the Grand Citadel."
Legendary tier shield.

Does 175-300 damage.
Range: 3.2 tiles.
Shots hit multiple targets.
Shots pass through obstacles.
MP Cost: 125.
Cooldown: 0.5 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

While equipped:
+100 Maximum HP.
+50 Defense.
-15 Speed.

Titan's Wrath: Anytime you take damage you have a 20% chance to recover most of the damage taken.

Does not drop from enemies.
