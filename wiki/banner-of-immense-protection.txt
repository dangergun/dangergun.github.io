Banner of Immense Protection [0x69c9]
"Used to protect the ones most dear to its user."
Legendary tier banner.

MP Cost: 90.
Cooldown: 8.0 seconds.
Fame Bonus: 8%.

When used:
Within 3.5 tiles, empowers allies for 7 seconds. Stays active for 6 seconds.

While equipped:
+10 Defense.
+10 Wisdom.
+40 Protection.
-20 Restoration.

Templar's Gift: If your surge is above 5 upon using your banner you remove all negative conditions upon yourself.

Does not drop from enemies.
